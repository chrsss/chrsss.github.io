About Me

Hi! I am Christina. I am a Design Engineer. I graduated with an MEng Degree in Design Engineering, from Imperial College London. I am currently pursuing a PhD in Computer Science at the University of Cyprus (UCY), while I am also working as a Research Associate at CYENS Centre of Excellence (CoE). My research interests focus in the area of VR, AR and MR and their novel applications, enhanced with real-time, realistic virtual human interaction, through the use of state-of-the-art hardware and algorithms.
